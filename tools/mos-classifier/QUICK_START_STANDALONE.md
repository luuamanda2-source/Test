## ğŸ“Š What Gets Classified

The tool looks for keywords in "Any Other Comments" and assigns one of these categories:

1. **Label/Document Failure** - Blank labels, printing issues
2. **API/Platform Blockers** - System/API issues
3. **System Feature Failure** - Features not working
4. **Status Conflict (Shipped/OOS)** - Marked shipped but out of stock
5. **Mismatched Status/Stuck** - Status inconsistencies
6. **Missing/Incorrect Data** - Data issues
7. **Ineligible Cancellation/Change** - Cannot cancel/change
8. **Replacement/Logistics Error** - Shipping/replacement issues
9. **Financial/Claim/Refund** - Money-related issues
10. **Policy & Guidance Gap** - Questions about policies
11. **Unclassified** - Doesn't match any category
